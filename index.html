Introducing to the clubs of MIST

MIST Innovation Club

MIST Innovation Club is one of the active clubs you'll see at MIST. In this rapidly advancing era of science, the importance of having technical knowledge and working innovatively definitely has no bounds. Thus, with the noble purpose of branding MIST with science, MIST Innovation Club (MIC) has commenced its journey on 12 February 2020. Our club thrives to create a platform to inspire the students of MIST in achieving technical and also soft skills to build innovative products or solutions that will represent MIST to the nation. Our eminent maxim is to “Innovate to Serve”.

MIST Computer Club

MIST Computer Club is one of the finest clubs of MIST that is supervised by our experienced, cooperative and so many helpful faculty members. Their guidance and support help us, students, a lot to enrich our club and hopefully we will do more good deeds in the future. MCC mainly focuses on contest programming and grooming students for competitive and professional programming. In 2020, MIST Computer Club hosted the National Collegiate Programming Contest (NCPC), the flagship programming contest in Bangladesh with participation from 77 universities.

MIST Robotics Club

Robotics involves design, construction, operation and use of robots. The mission of a Robotics Club is to spread knowledge about robotics and other robotics related aspects. We, MIST Robotics Club, try to do so by spreading knowledge about electric motor circuitry, electronics, micro controller and programming. The aim of the club is to become a platform for all student who are interested in robotics to share, combine and spread the knowledge and work together towards greater endeavors.

MIST Drama and Film Society

MIST Drama and Film Society (MDFS) is an extra-curricular activity platform related to Drama and Film for the students of MIST. This club was founded originally in 2011 by Shamim Hasan Sarkar and his fellow mates. It started as a Facebook group by a bunch of drama and film enthusiasts. But it started official journey in 2016-2017. At this transition period, to give this club a national platform our ex-president Md. Mobassir Hossain Irteza and club panel decided to arrange our very first national event. The event was “Bioscope”, a short film and standup comedy competition which was organized in 2017. It was a huge success and gave the decedent.

MIST Debating Club

Embarking on its journey since 2011, MIST Debating Society (MIST DS) has been actively contributing in the extracurricular activities of the students of MIST. Over the years, it has evolved as one of the significant contributors in achieving recognition for MIST, on both national and international grounds. This club helps students prepare well for debating, public speaking, idea competitions- which includes brainstorming and keeping oneself updated about the ongoing issues of the world. It also helps a student prepare well for different presentations, interviews etc in both engineering and non- engineering sectors, which become a vital part of life in the long run.

MIST Career Club

With a view to getting the students all prepared for obtaining their career destinations, MIST Career Club (MCC) works as a potential platform by engaging them in various career and self-development activities, since it’s beginning from 2017. Through organizing numbers of grooming sessions, seminars, workshops with the presence of mentors and representatives from country’s leading corporations, MIST Career Club does not only create ample inter-personal skill nourishment opportunities, but also establish a mutual collaborative relation for a longer term, with the ultimate goal of bridging the skills gap between campus and professional life.
