About Us

A Canvas where you see the exciting part of MIST, THE CLUBS

Mission

In “All Clubs MIST”, we collaborate with all the clubs by portraying their mission, vision, activities, and achievements.

Vision

We aim to create an environment for all the clubs and their members to come to one place and represent MIST together.


Our Story

Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST) is a playground of 15 different clubs. All working for the same vision i.e., to uphold the reputation of the institute and to provide its students with an environment in which they will learn and develop valuable skills by networking with like-minded people.

For this reason, a common place was necessary for all the clubs to share their common interests. This website is the result of that goal. A bunch of visionaries, talented and dedicated students of MIST came up with an idea to integrate all the clubs together in one website. Our clubs are the life of MIST. And this is the place where we all (the clubs) meet together.
